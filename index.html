About me

Hello, my name is Dilmurod Bukharov, I moved to the United States in the Summer of 2018 and since then I fell in love with this beautiful country and great people. I graduated Moscow State University of Steel and Alloys in Moscow, Russia and have both Bachelor’s and Master’s degree in metallurgical engineering. I am an intellectually curious person and love spending most of my spare time surfing the Internet and learning new things. I was always fascinated by web technologies and how it works but never really had a chance to learn it thoroughly. After I moved to the United States, I decided to change my career path and learn Software Development. I knew that changing my career would be challenging but I also was sure that it would be extremely rewarding. Finally, when I found out about General Assembly, I decided to enroll to one of their Software Engineering Immersive cohorts. So far, my journey into web development has been great and very interesting, I gained so much experience by working on real client projects and my own individual work.
